Privacy Policy

Transparency is the foundation of the TradeHut ecosystem. This policy outlines how we handle your information with architectural precision and mathematical honesty.

schedule LAST MODIFIED: 2023-10-24 14:32:00 UTC

Introduction

At TradeHut, operating under the Transparency Hub framework, we believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a checkbox. Our platform is designed to facilitate high-frequency trading and kinetic gallery experiences while maintaining total data integrity.

This Privacy Policy describes how TradeHut collects, uses, and shares personal information of users of our website and services. By using our platform, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Information We Collect

Personal Identifiers

Full name, business address, professional email, and encrypted financial credentials for auction processing.

Kinetic Data

Behavioral patterns within our gallery interface, including zoom levels, bidding speed, and viewing duration.

Technical Specification

  • check_circle Standard metadata: IP address, browser type, and domain names.
  • check_circle Log files: All high-value transactions are logged with millisecond precision (JetBrains Mono formatting).

How We Use Data

Your data is the fuel for our Kinetic Gallery engine. We process information to:

  • 01

    Execute Transactions

  • 02

    Interface Optimization

Engineered for absolute trust.

Your Privacy Rights

Right to Access

Request a full export of all personal data held within our systems, including log fragments.

Right to Erasure

The "Right to be Forgotten" applies to all non-transactional metadata after account closure.

We secure your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. TradeHut secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment.
