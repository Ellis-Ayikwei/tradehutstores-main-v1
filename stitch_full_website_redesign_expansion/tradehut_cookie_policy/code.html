Cookie Policy

At TradeHut, we believe in radical transparency. Our "Kinetic Gallery" experience is designed to be as seamless and high-fidelity as possible. This policy explains how we use cookies to ensure that performance.

1. Introduction

To deliver the TradeHut experience—a Kinetic Gallery that bridges the gap between high-frequency trading and editorial curation—we utilize various tracking technologies. These tools are essential for maintaining the architectural precision of our interface and the security of your high-value transactions.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that allow us to "remember" you. In the context of TradeHut, they function as the mathematical foundation for your session data. Think of them as the metadata that allows our Kinetic Engine to render real-time bids without latency.
