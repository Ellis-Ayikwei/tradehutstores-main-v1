The "No-Line" Philosophy

We reject the standard 1px border. We use tonal nesting and architectural spacing to create hierarchy. Here is how we layer the TradeHut experience.

Layer Level 0 & 1

Tonal Nesting

Content sits on cards (Surface Lowest), which sit on sections (Surface Container Low), which sit on the global background (Surface).

Ambient Occlusion

Atmospheric Shadows

We avoid harsh black shadows. Our cards use multi-layered occlusions using the 'on-surface' color at ultra-low opacities.
